An unknown jedi defeats Kylo Ren and takes his lightsaber

A powerful young jedi has been hunting Kylo Ren for years now. He tracked Ren to his base in the middle of a forest. The young jedi and Kylo Ren begin to fight. Ren taunts his opponent making him even angrier. He uses the anger to defeat Ren and take his light saber. It is later said that the jedi's parents were killed by Ren and he was seeking revenge.
